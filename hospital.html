Fortis Escorts Heart Institute, Delhi

Location: New Delhi Multi-Superspeciality Established In : 1988 Beds : 285 Accreditation : JCI, NABH

Fortis Escorts is a super-specialty quaternary care cardiac hospital and specializes in heart surgeries. Established in 1988, Fortis Escorts is a pioneer in cardiac surgery in India. The hospital has gained recognition the world over as the centre of excellence for its clinical outcomes. The hospital has the most advanced technology and has seen the best outcomes even in the most complex cardiac cases; be it cardiac surgery, Interventional Cardiology, Paediatric Cardiology, Pediatric Cardiac Surgery, or Non-invasive Cardiology. The institute is backed by the most advanced laboratories and diagnostic facilities performing a complete range of investigations.

Medanta Medicity, Gurgaon

Location: Gurgaon Multi-Superspeciality Established In : 2009 Beds : 1250 Accreditation : JCI, NABH, NABL

Dr. Naresh Trehan, a renowned Cardiovascular and Cardiothoracic surgeon, established Medanta Hospital in 2009. The Heart Institute, Institute of Neurosciences, Bone & Joint Institute, Kidney & Urology Institute, Cancer Institute, and Division of Medical Oncology and Hematology are all centres of expertise at Medanta. It is the country's first hospital to provide robotic surgery in cardiology, urology, and gynecology. In 2010, Asia's First Bloodless Bone Marrow Transplant HealthCare Global Enterprises (HCG) named Medanta the Best Multispecialty Hospital. In 2013, they won the VCCircle Healthcare Awards for Single Specialty Healthcare Entity.

Asian Heart Institute, Mumbai

Location: Mumbai Cardiology & Cardiac Surgery Established In : 2002 Beds : 250 Accreditation : JCI

Asian Heart Institute (AHI) was founded in 2002 and is among India's best heart care facilities, providing world-class cardiac care. More than 300,000 patients have been treated at the hospital, with 20,000 heart operations and 35,000 angiograms performed. The hospital is a world leader in the treatment of difficult heart surgery, with an astounding success rate of 99.8% in bypass surgeries and 99.4% in cardiac surgeries, the highest in the world.

Fortis Hospital, Noida

Location: Delhi (NCR) Multi-Superspeciality Established In : 2004 Beds : 200 Accreditation : NABH

The NABH accredited hospital of Fortis group in Noida was started in 2004 and is the only Fortis Escorts' Heart Command Centre in the entire UP. It is the only corporate facility in the area that offers a full range of services, from diagnostics to organ transplantation (kidney and liver). Cardiac, Neurological, Gastroenterological, and Renal Joint Therapeutic Services are available. The Cardiac Centre of Excellence at Fortis Noida has earned a great reputation because of its exceptional team and the results even in the most complex cardiac cases. Renal Science has risen to become one of the most important referral centres.

Narayana Health City, Hosur Road, Bangalore

Location: Bangalore Cardiac Established In : 2000 Beds : 700 Accreditation : JCI, NABH, NABL

Headquartered in Bangalore, Narayana Health City is the brainchild of world-renowned Paediatric Cardiac surgeon, Dr. Devi Shetty to bring quality healthcare within the reach of the needy and poor. From one hospital with 225 beds to around 6000 beds in three decades has been a remarkable journey for the enterprise. Narayana Health City, popularly known as Narayana Hrudyalya or NH in India, is among the strongest and most emerging names in the healthcare landscape in India. Narayana Health has a strong presence all over India.

Indraprastha Apollo Hospital, New Delhi

Location: New Delhi Multi-Superspeciality Established In : 1996 Beds : 1000 Accreditation : JCI, NABH

Indraprastha Apollo, New Delhi is a multi-specialty tertiary-care facility and is one of the most sought-after healthcare destinations in Asia. Indraprastha Apollo was the first hospital in India to be accredited by JCI. Spread over 15 acres, Apollo Delhi is one of the most modern and state-of-the-art facilities situated in the posh South Delhi. Considered to be the flagship hospital of Apollo group, Indraprastha Apollo Hospital is one of the important landmarks not only in Delhi but on the world map because of its popularity among medical tourists. Spread over 15 acres, Indraprastha Apollo has a built-up area of over 600,000 square feet. The hospital offers more than 50 specialties under one roof.
